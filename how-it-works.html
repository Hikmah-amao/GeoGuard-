How GeoGuard Works

1. Data Collection

GeoGuard collects satellite imagery, environmental data, and terrain information from open sources like NASA and AWS Earth datasets

2. AI & Geospatial Analysis

Using machine learning models and GIS tools, the system analyzes patterns to detect floods, illegal mining sites, and groundwater locations

3. Real-Time Monitoring & Alerts

The platform displays results through a web dashboard and sends alerts to users in at-risk areas. Future versions will include SMS and offline options

Community Actions

With the data provided, local governments, NGOs, and communities can respond quickly to disasters, plan safe mining, and access water more sustainibly.
